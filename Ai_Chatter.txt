* Modulation Frequency: $100\text{ kHz to } 250\text{ kHz}$.
* The Blueprint: The voltage across the electrodes is modulated as a sawtooth waveform. A sharp, near-vertical voltage spike instantly grabs the ionized plasma packet via the Lorentz force, followed by a linear decay ramp. This high-frequency pulsing prevents the plasma from developing localized thermal instabilities (arcing) and distributes the physical thrust force evenly down the length of the accelerator channel.
